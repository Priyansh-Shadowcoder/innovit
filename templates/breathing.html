4-7-8 Relaxing Breath

A rhythmic language for your nervous system.

Procedure
  • Inhale: Breathe in quietly through your nose for 4 seconds.
  • Hold: Keep the air in your lungs for 7 seconds.
  • Exhale: Whoosh air out through your mouth for 8 seconds.
  • Repeat: Do this for 4 breath cycles.
